## If we have submitted for a PA, completed a P2P, and otherwise exhausted our options:

1.	Melissa will review the PA Response from the insurance company. If we receive a **full denial**, Melissa will upload the documentation to the client’s Google Drive and flag the client using the Insurance Review process. 

2.	Andrew will review the documentation to ensure that there isn’t anything else we can do. If we can do something, we stop this procedure. Otherwise:

3.	Melissa will call the client to inform them that we were unable to get insurance approval. We can usually (on a case-by-case basis) offer the Private Pay route or recommend another evaluation center (MUSC, if appropriate, Charleston Neuropsych, etc.). 

4.	Melissa will document the Info tab of the EMR will specific details of the conversation with the family. Indicate whether PP was offered and/or what the family said in response to our offers.

5.	Follow our closing procedures to mark clients Inactive, archive documentation, etc.
